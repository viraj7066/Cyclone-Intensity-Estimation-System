Hamoon was a cyclone that occurred between October 23-25, 2023, in the Bay of Bengal. It had a maximum wind speed of 148 km/h, with on-land speeds reaching up to 137 km/h. The cyclone had a diameter of 370 km and an air pressure below 978 mbar. Classified as a category 1 cyclone on the Saffir-Simpson scale.
